Maushumi is a First Generation award winning Entrepreneur from Jharkhand. This passionate gutsy woman took on tough illness head on, and fought back with grit and fortitude. In her quest to find a natural solution, she stumbled upon Ayurveda, the ancient science of natural healing.

After closely practising the craft for more than six years. Maushumi was astounded by the results. Wanting to pass on the wisdom for the benefit of many others, Maushumi started MrilQ. She quickly became a household name in India and abroad recalled for her quality products.

MrilQ today is a way of life not only for Maushumi but also for hundreds of people who are its patrons. They are benefitting from it in numerous ways. Read how clients are benefitting from MrilQ

Our Eco-Values

At MrilQ we abide by our Eco-Values for everything we do. These values include:
  • Authentically Organic Formulas: Made With Certified Organic & Organically Grown Ingredients to enhance the powerful antioxidants and to nullify pesticide and synthetic fertilizer residue.
  • Powerful Action Without using Potentially Harmful Ingredients: formulated without parabens, petroleum, pesticides, propylene or butylene glycols, silicones, talc, carbon black, phthalates, sulfates, PEGs, TEA, DEA, GMO, silicones, artificial dyes or synthetic fragrances. And for knowing what key ingredients we add, go to MrilQ ingredients.
  • Vegan & No Animal Testing: formulated without animal byproducts, PETA compliant and awarded "fully natural" in Commerce.
  • Sustainable Company Practices: Utilizing Solar & Wind Power & Sourcing Locally: Many of MrilQ’s products are made with sustainable energy and our boxes are made with recycled or FSC certified paper, printed with soy/non-toxic ink & sourced in the pristine hills of Jharkhand. Our corporate headquarters contains all sustainable/re-used materials. The Company focuses on sustainable practices from top to bottom. Socially Responsible Giving: Long term support and partnership with The Breast Cancer Prevention Partners & EWG/ Environmental Working Group.
