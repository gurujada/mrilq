Q Can I use products from MrilQ Cosmeceuticals if I have sensitive skin?

A. MrilQ is made up of Natural & Organically sourced botanical Ingredients, free from the most common allergens such as SLS, artificial colours and fragrances. However, everyone’s skin is unique and it would be impossible for us to predict about how your skin will react. Some people may find they are sensitive to natural ingredients such as oils or fruit extracts found in natural products and for this reason we display the full product ingredients within the product page of our website to enable customers to make an informed choice before purchase. Even the most ‘gentle’ ingredients can cause a reaction in certain people’s skin so we recommend patch-testing a small amount of the product on the inside skin of the wrist area to ascertain its compatibility.

Q How do I carry out a patch test?

A. The inside skin of the wrist is the most effective area on which to carry out a patch test. You can also test on the skin behind your ear if you suspect only your face is sensitive. Simply apply the product to that area and if any irritation occurs, often showing as a rash, a burning, prickly or tingling sensation, redness or any other temporary discoloration of the skin, wash with water or a damp cloth.

Q What if I have an allergic reaction to a product?

A. If you have carried out a patch test, as explained in how do I carry out a patch test, and have experienced an allergic reaction, please do not use the product.

Q Can I use MrilQ products if I am pregnant?

A.MrilQ is made from 100% natural botanical ingredients. Products are tested and certified by National Test House Govt. of India & NAB Labs to be 100% chemical free. So they are totally safe to be used by expecting & lactating mothers.

However please consult your doctor for further advice if you should use the products.
